Why Chronic Pain Persists

The Missing Link Most Doctors Overlook

Chronic pain doesn't just live in joints or discs — it lives in the neurological environment those structures exist within.

🧠

Pain Is Treated Like a Wiring Problem

Most approaches focus on structural findings. But if the central nervous system remains inflamed, it continues amplifying signals regardless of local treatments.

🔍

Neuroinflammation Is Invisible

It doesn't reliably appear on standard imaging, leaving patients hypersensitized despite "normal" scans and test results.

⚡

It Affects Your Entire Life

Central inflammation contributes to fatigue, brain fog, stress sensitivity, and depressive symptoms — far beyond just physical pain.
